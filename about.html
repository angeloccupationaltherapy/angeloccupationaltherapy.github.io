Our mission statement

Mrs. Shangmiaw Sheu, the founder, is a registered occupational therapist (OTR) and licensed in the State of Califomia. She is a member of the American Occupational Therapy Association. Mrs. Sheu received her Bachelor of Science degree in occupational therapy from the National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan and Master of Science degree in occupational therapy from the State University of New York at Buffalo, Buffalo, NY. She is also trained in the comprehensive program of sensory integration including administration of Sensory Integration and Praxis Tests (SIPT).

With more than twenty-two years of experience working with children with special needs at schools, early intervention programs and day training centers, Mrs. Sheu is committed to providing her clients with excellent therapy and personal attention. She has a great passion for her profession and treats all her clients with much patience and care.

Please contact her at (626) 284-6888 or use the "Contact Us" form on this website if you would like to schedule a clinic visit or if you have any questions. Mrs. Sheu speaks English, Chinese, and Taiwanese.
